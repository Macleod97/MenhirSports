ABOUT

Based in the centre of the largest national park in the UK, Menhir Sports is passionate about the outdoors and we aim to make outdoor activities accessible to everyone. The Cairngorms National Park is one of the rare places within the British Isles where people can experience a vast variety of environments in one place.

Not only do Menhir Sports want to encourage outdoor activity and enjoyment of the beautiful landscapes that the Scottish Highlands have to offer but we are also very concern about the environment and maintaining the beauty that exists within nature.

This is why we have collaborated with forestry charities to help maintain the vast woodlands within the Highlands and have adopted the use of alternative energy in all of our activity centres.

We love nature and are striving to keep it beautiful and accessible.
